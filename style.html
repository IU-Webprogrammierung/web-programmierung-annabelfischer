essentials

heart shaped, denim and lip gloss

stylish essentials are the building blocks of a chic and versatile wardrobe, reflecting both individual taste and timeless elegance. a heart-shaped accessory adds a touch of whimsy and romance to any ensemble, serving as a playful yet sophisticated statement piece. denim, with its effortless cool and versatility, is a wardrobe staple that effortlessly transitions from day to night, casual to chic. lip gloss, with its subtle sheen and hint of color, is the perfect finishing touch, adding a touch of glamour and polish to any look. and gold jewelry, with its timeless allure and luxurious shine, elevates any outfit, lending a touch of sophistication and refinement to every occasion. together, these stylish essentials form the foundation of a fashion-forward wardrobe, effortlessly blending trendsetting style with classic sophistication.

but one last word: wear and buy whatever makes you the happiest

featured style

full denim outfit

a full denim outfit, infused with 70s-inspired flair, epitomizes effortless cool and retro charm. starting with high-waisted flared jeans, the silhouette elongates the legs while nodding to the iconic style of the disco era. paired with a cropped denim jacket adorned with patchwork or embroidery, the ensemble exudes vintage allure with a contemporary twist. completing the look, a fitted denim shirt or blouse adds a touch of tailored sophistication, while oversized round sunglasses channel the free-spirited vibes of the 1970s. this ensemble celebrates the timeless appeal of denim while embracing the nostalgia of a bygone era, effortlessly blending retro chic with modern sensibility.

annabel fischer, 2024
